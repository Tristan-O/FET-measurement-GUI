Connected Devices

Can copy/paste the "level" for instruments as a list from excel. The string will be split automatically (whitespace, commas or semicolons). Instruments will iterate over the elements of the applied level and will wrap back to the beginning (treated as periodic) when the Start button is pressed. However, any resulting element that cannot be typecasted in a float (e.g. !) will be treated as a STOP order. Pause will pause the iteration without resetting the place in the iteration. Stop will also reset the place in the iteration.
